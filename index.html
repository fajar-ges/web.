Profil Perpustakaan Gotham

Gotham City Library is where Barbara Gordon works as a librarian. Batman and Robin then confront Killer Moth and his three henchmen in the library. During No Man's Land, the library is taken over by the Riddler, who uses the building as his base for an entire year. At one point, the library was raided by Eduardo Dorrance and James Gordon when they tried to arrest Nygma for destroying the Haven building in the Green Zone. [4] Several weeks later, the Gotham City Public Library was also the site of a violent confrontation between the Penguin and the Ventriloquist .
